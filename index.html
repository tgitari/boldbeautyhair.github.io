Synthetic Hair vs. Human Hair: Which One is Best?

The first thing you should know hair extensions and wigs is that there are two types of hair used to produce these accessories –synthetic hair or human hair. On occasion, you may even bump into a wig that is of a blend between the two. The common question in the great debate about hair extensions and wigs is (drum roll) –Which type of is better –synthetic or human hair? The Short Answer: Human hair is better than synthetic hair due to quality, versatility, and lifespan.

Hair extensions and wigs are a girl’s best friend when you think about the many advantages this accessory delivers. You can add length, volume, color and even manage to turn a few heads with the right style and dash of hair extension fashion flair.

Frontals vs. Closures: Which One Should I Wear?

Should you wear a lace closure or frontal? Do not minimize the significance of this question. If you are not looking to switch up your style too often, lace closures are great. You can position the closure so that your part rests down the middle of your head or you can install the closure so that the part rests along the side of your head. Styling versatility will be limited, but convenience will be off the charts. Frontals are fun, and these installations are typically done using an adhesive –however, there are creative ways to secure a frontalwithout using glue. Frontals become your faux hairline, spanning the perimeter of your head from ear to ear. Unlike closures, these pieces allow for multiple styling optionsincluding updos and movement of your hair parts. Both frontals and closures use lace or silk as their base to create the illusion of hair growing from your scalp or hairline.

What is a Bundle of Hair?

Finally, a bundle of hair is a single weft of hair extension. Bundles range in lengths of 10” to 32”. Hair origin may vary including Malaysian, Indian, or Brazilian. Also, you have the option of choosing multiple colors including brunette, browns, blondes, and reds.
